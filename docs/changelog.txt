# ReflectivEI Changelog

## 2025-10-09 — Initial Release

- **Initial release** of the ReflectivEI AI Sales Enablement Platform.  
- **Site structure** established with `index.html`, `styles.css`, `script.js`, and supporting assets.  
- **Implemented AI Chat Widget** with three functional modes:  
  - *Emotional Assessment*  
  - *HIV Product Knowledge*  
  - *Sales Simulation*  
- **Added healthcare provider scenarios** for simulation practice and integrated basic coach feedback generation.  
- **Added** `about-ei.md`, summarizing Emotional Assessment protocols and HIV medication information.  
- **Created** system, persona, and configuration files (`system.md`, `persona.json`, `config.json`) for AI context.  
- **Published documentation** under `/docs`, including `maintenance.md` and `integration-notes.md`.
