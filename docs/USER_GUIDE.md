## Using the Feedback Coach

### Opening the Feedback Coach

**Method 1: From Homepage**
1. Click "Explore Platform" button
2. The Feedback Coach modal opens

**Method 2: Widget Button**
1. Look for the floating "Feedback Coach" button (bottom-right corner)
2. Click to open the coaching interface

### Feedback Coach Interface

**Components:**
- **Mode Selector** - Choose your coaching mode
- **Chat Input** - Type your message or question
- **Send Button** - Submit for AI analysis
- **Response Area** - View AI coaching feedback
- **Close Button** - Exit the coach

### Basic Workflow

1. **Select a Mode**
   - Click the mode dropdown
   - Choose: Role-Play, Sales Coach, Emotional Intelligence, or Product Knowledge

2. **Enter Your Message**
   - Type a question, scenario, or message
   - Be specific for better feedback

3. **Send and Review**
   - Click "Send" or press Enter
   - Wait for AI analysis (usually 2-5 seconds)
   - Review the coaching feedback

4. **Iterate and Improve**
   - Apply the feedback
   - Try again with refined messaging
   - Track your improvement

## Coaching Modes

### 1. Role-Play Mode

**Purpose:** Practice HCP conversations in a safe environment

**How to Use:**
1. Select "Role-Play" from the mode dropdown
2. Describe the scenario (e.g., "I'm meeting a cardiologist about our new PCSK9 inhibitor")
3. The AI becomes the HCP
4. Engage in a realistic conversation
5. Receive feedback on your approach

**Best For:**
- Pre-call preparation
- Handling difficult situations
- Building confidence
- Testing new messaging

**Example Scenario:**
```
User: I'm visiting Dr. Smith, a skeptical cardiologist. I want to discuss our PCSK9 inhibitor.

AI (as Dr. Smith): I've been using statins successfully for decades. Why should I consider changing?

User: [Practice your response]

AI: [Provides feedback on your handling of the objection]
```

### 2. Sales Coach Mode

**Purpose:** Get real-time coaching on sales conversations and messaging

**How to Use:**
1. Select "Sales Coach" mode
2. Choose your therapeutic area (if prompted)
3. Ask for coaching on specific topics:
   - Message refinement
   - Objection handling
   - Value proposition
   - Call planning

**Best For:**
- Refining your messaging
- Preparing for tough questions
- Understanding competitive positioning
- Developing call strategies

**Example Questions:**
- "How should I position our product against the standard of care?"
- "What's the best way to handle the price objection?"
- "How do I explain the MOA to a busy HCP?"

### 3. Emotional Intelligence (EI) Mode

**Purpose:** Develop your emotional intelligence and relationship skills

**How to Use:**
1. Select "Emotional Intelligence" mode
2. Engage in a conversation
3. Review the **EI Panel** that appears with your response
4. Click any EI dimension pill to see detailed feedback

**EI Dimensions Scored:**
1. **Empathy Recognition** - Understanding HCP emotions
2. **Active Listening** - Attentiveness to concerns
3. **Emotional Adaptability** - Adjusting to emotional shifts
4. **Clarity** - Clear communication
5. **Resilience** - Handling rejection
6. **Action Insight** - Practical next steps
7. **Confidence** - Poise and assertiveness
8. **Discovery** - Asking the right questions
9. **Objection Handling** - Addressing concerns
10. **Compliance** - Regulatory adherence

**EI Panel Features:**
- **Color-coded pills** - Instant visual feedback
- **Scores** - Numerical ratings (0-100 or 0-10)
- **Click to expand** - Detailed rationale for each score
- **Actionable advice** - How to improve

**Example:**
```
User: The doctor seems frustrated with the prior authorization process.

AI Response: [Coaching feedback]

EI Panel Shows:
- Empathy Recognition: 95% (Green) - You identified frustration
- Active Listening: 88% (Green) - Good acknowledgment
- Emotional Adaptability: 72% (Yellow) - Could adjust tone more
[Click any pill for detailed feedback]
```

### 4. Product Knowledge Mode

**Purpose:** Learn and reinforce therapeutic area knowledge

**How to Use:**
1. Select "Product Knowledge" mode
2. Choose a therapeutic area:
   - Cardiovascular
   - COVID-19
   - HIV/AIDS
   - Oncology
   - (More available)
3. Ask questions about:
   - Disease pathophysiology
   - Product mechanism of action
   - Clinical data
   - Safety profiles
   - Competitive landscape

**Best For:**
- Learning new therapeutic areas
- Preparing for launches
- Refreshing knowledge
- Understanding clinical data

**Example Questions:**
- "What's the MOA of PCSK9 inhibitors?"
- "Explain the REDUCE-IT trial results"
- "How does our product compare to competitor X?"
- "What are the key safety concerns for this class?"

**Important:** All information is label-accurate and compliant with regulatory guidelines.

## Best Practices

### General Tips

1. **Be Specific**
   - Provide detailed scenarios
   - Include context (therapeutic area, HCP type, situation)
   - Ask focused questions

2. **Practice Regularly**
   - Use the platform before important calls
   - Dedicate 15-30 minutes daily
   - Review and apply feedback consistently

3. **Use Multiple Modes**
   - Start with Product Knowledge to learn
   - Move to Role-Play to practice
   - Use Sales Coach for refinement
   - Track progress with EI mode

4. **Focus on One Skill at a Time**
   - Don't try to improve everything at once
   - Pick one EI dimension to develop
   - Master it, then move to the next

### Mode-Specific Tips

**Role-Play:**
- Start with common scenarios
- Gradually increase difficulty
- Practice the same scenario multiple times
- Track your improvement

**Sales Coach:**
- Be specific about your challenge
- Ask for multiple approaches
- Request examples and scripts
- Apply immediately in the field

**Emotional Intelligence:**
- Pay attention to all 10 dimensions
- Click pills to understand scores
- Focus on lower-scoring areas
- Practice empathy in every interaction

**Product Knowledge:**
- Review before launch meetings
- Use for competitive intelligence
- Confirm your understanding
- Ask for clinical data interpretation

### Compliance Best Practices

**Always:**
- ✅ Stay on-label
- ✅ Reference approved materials
- ✅ Verify claims with citations
- ✅ Use compliant language

**Never:**
- ❌ Discuss off-label uses
- ❌ Make unsupported claims
- ❌ Share non-approved materials
- ❌ Dismiss safety concerns

**ReflectivAI Compliance Features:**
- Automated guardrails
- Label-only content
- Citation system
- Compliance scoring (96%)

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is ReflectivAI free to use?**
A: The platform offers a free trial for individuals. Enterprise licenses are available for organizations. Contact sales@reflectivai.com for pricing.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: For basic exploration, no account is needed. For full access, tracking, and team features, an account is required.

**Q: Can I use ReflectivAI on my mobile phone?**
A: Yes! The platform is fully responsive and works on all devices.

**Q: How is my data used?**
A: Your conversations are used to improve AI coaching. No personal health information is collected. See our Privacy Policy for details.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why is the Feedback Coach slow to respond?**
A: Response time depends on:
- Your internet connection speed
- Current server load
- Complexity of your query
Typical response time is 2-5 seconds.

**Q: The widget won't load. What should I do?**
A: Try:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
5. Contact support if the issue persists

**Q: Can I embed the widget on my own website?**
A: Yes! See the [API Reference](API_REFERENCE.md) for embed code and instructions.

### Feature Questions

**Q: How often are metrics updated?**
A: Currently, metrics shown are representative scores. Real-time tracking coming in Phase 5.

**Q: Can I export my coaching conversations?**
A: This feature is planned for a future release.

**Q: What therapeutic areas are covered?**
A: Currently:
- Cardiovascular disease
- COVID-19
- HIV/AIDS
- Oncology
Additional areas being added regularly.

**Q: How does the EI scoring work?**
A: Each conversation is analyzed across 10 dimensions using advanced natural language processing and AI. Scores are based on:
- Language patterns
- Emotional awareness markers
- Compliance indicators
- Clinical accuracy
- Communication clarity

**Q: Are the scores accurate?**
A: Scores are based on AI analysis of your conversations. They are directional indicators, not absolute measurements. Use them to track trends and identify areas for improvement.

### Coaching Questions

**Q: How realistic are the role-play scenarios?**
A: Very realistic. The AI is trained on thousands of real HCP conversations and can simulate various personality types, objections, and scenarios.

**Q: Can I practice with a specific HCP persona?**
A: Yes! In Role-Play mode, describe the HCP type (e.g., "skeptical cardiologist," "early adopter oncologist") and the AI will adapt.

**Q: What if the AI gives me advice I disagree with?**
A: The AI provides guidance based on best practices, but you know your territory best. Use the coaching as input, not absolute truth. Discuss concerns with your manager.

**Q: How do I know if I'm improving?**
A: Look for:
- Higher EI dimension scores over time
- Positive feedback patterns from the AI
- Better handling of objections
- Increased confidence in calls
- Better field results
