How Your Scores Are Calculated

ReflectivAI uses a multi-layered assessment approach that combines:

  • AI-Driven Analysis: Advanced language models analyze your communication for empathy markers, clarity indicators, compliance signals, and emotional regulation patterns.
  • Rubric-Based Evaluation: Structured rubrics score specific behaviors against best practices in sales enablement and emotional intelligence.
  • Deterministic Heuristics: Explainable, rule-based scoring that identifies affective language patterns, acknowledgment phrases, pauses, and tone indicators.
  • Triple-Loop Reflection: Assessment across task outcome (Loop 1), emotional regulation (Loop 2), and mindset reframing (Loop 3).

All scoring is transparent and explainable—no black-box algorithms. Your feedback includes specific rationales tied to observable behaviors.
