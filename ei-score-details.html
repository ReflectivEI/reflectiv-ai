Understanding Your EI Scores

ReflectivAI evaluates your emotional intelligence across six core domains based on evidence-based frameworks from Goleman, Mayer‚ÄìSalovey‚ÄìCaruso, and CASEL's SEL competencies. Each domain measures specific behavioral markers and communication patterns.

EI Domain Definitions

Empathy

--

Ability to acknowledge perspectives, demonstrate emotional attunement, and use phrases like "I hear your concern" or "It makes sense that..."

Objection Handling

--

Skill in addressing concerns constructively, reframing objections, and maintaining composure under challenge.

Clarity & Confidence

--

Concise communication, limited jargon, clear structure, and confident delivery without overloading the listener.

Accuracy & Compliance

--

Label-safe phrasing, fact-based claims, ethical framing, and adherence to regulatory standards.

Discovery

--

Quality of inquiry, asking open-ended questions, uncovering needs, and collaborative dialogue.

Self-Regulation

--

Neutral tone, pauses before responding, emotional control, and maintaining composure in difficult interactions.

EI ‚Üî SEL Competency Mapping

Our six EI domains map to the five Social & Emotional Learning (SEL) competencies:

  • Self-Awareness: Discovery, Self-Regulation
  • Self-Management: Clarity, Self-Regulation
  • Social Awareness: Empathy, Discovery
  • Relationship Skills: Empathy, Objection Handling
  • Responsible Decision-Making: Accuracy & Compliance

Each learner builds a Reflective Index that updates after every simulation using an exponential moving average. This ensures your scores reflect both recent performance and long-term growth trends.
