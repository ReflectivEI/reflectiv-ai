Ethics, Privacy & Governance

ReflectivAI is built for life-sciences training. We avoid PHI by default and apply strict safeguards when customers choose to integrate real data.

Data privacy & security
  • PHI off by default; training uses synthetic/de-identified data.
  • When PHI enabled: DPA + BAA, encryption in transit/at rest, RBAC, audit logs, timed purge.
  • Vendor risk management & annual reviews.
Informed consent
  • If real patient data powers enablement, obtain explicit, documented consent and clear opt-out.
Algorithmic bias & fairness
  • Pre-deployment bias tests; periodic retests.
  • Monitor disparate performance; publish remediation.
  • Targeting guardrails avoid non-clinical demographic proxies.
Transparency & explainability
  • In-product reason codes; model cards; change logs.
Human oversight & accountability
  • Human reviewers curate scenarios and rubrics; escalation paths for model error.
Doctor-patient relationship
  • Training supports HCP judgment; off-label promotion prohibited.

Frequently Asked Questions

What sets ReflectivAI apart?

ReflectivAI combines AI simulations and analytics with subtle, emotionally intelligent coaching cues — all within strict life-sciences compliance guardrails.

Does it replace human coaching?

No. ReflectivAI scales practice, insights, and certification; managers and trainers remain essential.

How do you handle PHI?

PHI is off by default. When enabled, we operate under HIPAA controls (minimum-necessary scope, encryption in transit/at rest, audit logging) and a signed BAA.

Is it customizable by disease state and region?

Yes. Modules, objections, and labeling align to your therapeutic areas and local regulations.

Can it integrate with LMS/CRM?

Export packs and APIs are available for Salesforce, Veeva, and leading LMS platforms.
