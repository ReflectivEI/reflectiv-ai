Ethics, Privacy & Governance

ReflectivAI is built for life-sciences training. We avoid PHI by default and apply strict safeguards when customers choose to integrate real data.

Data privacy & security
  • PHI is off by default; training uses synthetic or de-identified data.
  • When a customer enables PHI, we operate under HIPAA controls with a signed BAA; encryption in transit/at rest; role-based access; audit logs.
  • Vendor risk management includes annual security reviews and a right-to-audit clause.
Informed consent
  • If real patient data powers enablement, customers must obtain explicit, documented consent and provide clear opt-out.
Algorithmic bias & fairness
  • Pre-deployment bias testing; periodic re-tests.
  • Monitor disparate performance; publish remediation steps.
  • Guardrails prohibit non-clinical demographic targeting.
Transparency & explainability
  • In-product recommendations include reason codes.
  • Model cards document data sources, limitations, and cadence.
  • AI-generated content is clearly labeled.
Human oversight & accountability
  • Human reviewers curate scenario content and rubrics.
  • Defined escalation paths for model errors.
  • Versioning for MLR traceability.
Doctor–patient relationship
  • Training supports, not manipulates, HCP judgement.
  • Off-label promotion is prohibited; risky language flagged.
Compliance anchors

We align to HIPAA Privacy Rule, PhRMA Code, FDA OPDP, NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 27001, ISO/IEC 42001, and OECD AI Principles.

Frequently Asked Questions

What sets ReflectivAI apart?

AI simulations + analytics with subtle, emotionally-intelligent coaching cues — all inside strict life-sciences compliance guardrails.

Does it replace human coaching?

No. ReflectivAI scales practice, insights, and certification; managers remain essential to interpret data and personalize development.

How do you handle PHI?

PHI is off by default. When enabled, we operate under HIPAA controls and a signed BAA (minimum-necessary scope, encryption in transit/at rest, audit logging).
