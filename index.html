EI-Driven Coaching

The ReflectivAI Coach evaluates every exchange through an emotional intelligence lens, measuring not just what reps say, but how effectively they connect, listen, and adapt.

How the Coach Works

Each simulation exchange is analyzed across six critical dimensions: Accuracy (clinical correctness), Empathy (emotional resonance), Clarity (message simplicity), Compliance (label adherence), Discovery (uncovering needs), and Objection Handling (navigating resistance).

After every rep response, the Coach provides Suggested Phrasing‚Äîalternative ways to express the same idea with better tone, empathy, or precision. The What Worked / What to Improve panel breaks down strengths and growth areas, helping reps practice EI-driven conversations that build trust and drive action.

This isn't generic feedback. Every coaching moment ties back to emotional intelligence principles, teaching reps to regulate tone under pressure, listen for emotional cues, and respond with authentic empathy‚Äîall while staying compliant.

Compliance & Safety

Built-in guardrails and responsible AI practices that protect your brand while empowering reps to practice confidently.

ReflectivAI is purpose-built for regulated pharmaceutical environments. Every simulation operates within label-only coaching boundaries, with automated guardrails that prevent off-label drift in both the AI HCP persona and the Coach's feedback. This means reps can safely make mistakes, explore different approaches, and learn from failures‚Äîall without real-world compliance risk.

Our system continuously monitors for restricted content, flagging any exchange that approaches off-label territory before it becomes a training issue. The Coach guides reps back to approved messaging, reinforcing compliant language patterns through repetition and positive reinforcement.

Legal and compliance teams can review, approve, and version-control all scenarios through our MLR workflow. Every simulation is traceable, auditable, and aligned with your organization's compliance standards‚Äîgiving you peace of mind while your reps practice.

  • Label-Only Coaching: Automated detection and prevention of off-label content in all interactions
  • Restricted Off-Label Drift: Real-time guardrails flag and redirect conversations approaching restricted territory
  • HCP & Coach Guardrails: Both AI personas are constrained to operate within compliance boundaries
  • Safe Practice Space: Reps can experiment and make mistakes without real-world consequences

Compliance FAQ

ReflectivAI uses multi-layered content filters and real-time monitoring to detect off-label statements. The AI HCP persona is trained not to ask off-label questions, and the Coach immediately flags any rep response that approaches restricted territory, providing corrective guidance before it becomes a pattern.

All simulation data is stored in SOC 2-compliant, encrypted cloud infrastructure. PHI is disabled by default for most training scenarios. When PHI handling is required, we operate under BAA agreements with role-based access controls and audit logging. Data residency options are available for specific regulatory requirements.

Yes. ReflectivAI includes an MLR workflow where legal and compliance teams can review, comment on, and approve scenarios before deployment. All approved scenarios are version-controlled, and any updates require re-approval. You maintain full control over what content your reps practice with.
