Ethics, Privacy & Governance

Data privacy & security
  • PHI off by default; training uses synthetic or de-identified data.
  • When enabled, HIPAA BAA, encryption, RBAC, purge on expiration.
  • Vendor risk management with annual reviews and audit rights.
Transparency & explainability
  • In-product recommendations include reason codes; model cards document sources and limits.
Algorithmic bias & fairness
  • Pre-deployment bias testing and periodic re-tests.
  • Monitor disparate performance; publish remediation steps.
Human oversight & accountability
  • Humans curate scenarios and rubrics; MLR traceability for message libraries.
