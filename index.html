Ethics, Privacy & Governance

Data privacy & security

  • PHI off by default; de-identified data for training.
  • When enabled: BAA, encryption, RBAC, audit logs, purge on expiration.
  • Sub-processor vetting and annual reviews.

Informed consent

  • Explicit consent and opt-out if real data is used.

Bias & fairness

  • Pre/post-deployment tests and monitoring.

Transparency

  • Reason codes and model cards.

Oversight
