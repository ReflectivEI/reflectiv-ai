Ethics, Privacy & Governance

ReflectivAI is built for life-sciences training. We avoid PHI by default and apply strict safeguards when customers choose to integrate real data.

Data privacy & security
  • PHI is off by default; training uses synthetic or de-identified data.
  • When a customer enables PHI, we operate under BAA, encryption in transit/at rest, role-based access, and purge on expiration.
  • Vendor risk management with annual reviews and right-to-audit.
Informed consent
  • If real patient data powers enablement, customers obtain explicit, documented consent and provide an opt-out.
Algorithmic bias & fairness
  • Pre-deployment bias testing and periodic re-tests.
  • Monitor disparate performance; publish remediation steps.
  • Guardrails prohibit non-clinical proxies for targeting.
Transparency & explainability
  • In-product recommendations include reason codes.
  • Model cards document data sources, limitations, cadence.
  • AI-generated content clearly labeled.
Human oversight & accountability
  • Humans curate scenarios and rubrics; escalation paths defined.
  • All message libraries versioned for MLR traceability.
Doctor-patient relationship
  • Training supports, not manipulates, HCP judgement.
  • Off-label promotion prohibited; risky language flagged.
