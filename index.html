Ethics, Privacy & Governance

ReflectivAI is built for life-sciences training. We avoid Protected Health Information (PHI) by default and apply strict safeguards when customers choose to integrate real data.

Data privacy & security
  • PHI is off by default; training uses synthetic or de-identified data.
  • When a customer enables PHI, we operate under HIPAA controls and a signed BAA.
  • Vendor risk management includes security reviews and right-to-audit.
Informed consent
  • If real patient data is used, customers must obtain explicit consent with an opt-out.
Algorithmic bias & fairness
  • Pre-deployment bias tests; periodic re-tests.
  • Monitor disparate performance and document remediation.
Transparency & explainability
  • In-product recommendations include reason codes.
  • Model cards document data sources, limits and cadence.
Human oversight & accountability
  • Human reviewers curate scenarios and rubrics; escalation paths defined.
Doctor-patient relationship
  • Training supports HCP judgement; off-label promotion is prohibited.
