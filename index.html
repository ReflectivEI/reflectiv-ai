Ethics, Privacy & Governance

ReflectivAI is built for life-sciences training. We avoid PHI by default and apply strict safeguards when customers choose to integrate real data.

Data privacy & security

  • PHI is off by default; training uses synthetic or de-identified data.
  • When a customer enables PHI, we require DPA + BAA; data encrypted in transit/at rest; role-based access; audit logs retained; timed purge[1].
  • Vendor risk management: vetted sub-processors, annual reviews, right-to-audit.

Informed consent

  • If real patient data powers enablement, customers must obtain explicit, documented consent with opt-out.

Algorithmic bias & fairness

  • Pre-/post-deployment bias testing on representative datasets.
  • Monitor disparate performance; publish remediation steps.
  • Guardrails prohibit non-clinical demographic targeting.

Transparency & explainability

  • In-product recommendations include reason codes.
  • Model cards document data sources, limits, and cadence.
  • AI-generated content labeled throughout training materials.

Human oversight & accountability

  • Human reviewers curate scenarios and rubrics.
  • Clear escalation paths for model errors.
  • All versions are MLR-trackable.

Doctor–patient relationship

  • Training supports, not manipulates, HCP judgment.
  • Off-label promotion prohibited; risky language flagged.

Compliance anchors

We align with: HIPAA Privacy Rule, PhRMA Code, FDA OPDP, NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 27001, ISO/IEC 42001, OECD AI Principles.
