Ethics, Privacy & Governance

ReflectivAI is built for life‑sciences training. We avoid Protected Health Information (PHI) by default and apply strict safeguards when customers choose to integrate real data.

Data privacy & security

  • PHI is off by default; training uses synthetic or de‑identified data.
  • When a customer enables PHI, we require a signed data‑processing agreement and a HIPAA business associate agreement. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, access is role‑based, audit logs are retained and data is purged on expiration[1].
  • Vendor risk management: we vet sub‑processors for equivalent safeguards and conduct annual security reviews with a right‑to‑audit clause.

Informed consent

  • If real patient data is used to power enablement, customers must obtain explicit, documented consent distinct from clinical consent and provide a clear opt‑out option.

Algorithmic bias & fairness

  • We conduct pre‑deployment bias testing on representative datasets and repeat tests periodically.
  • We monitor for disparate performance across specialties, geographies and demographic contexts and publish remediation steps.
  • Sales targeting guardrails prohibit using non‑clinical demographic proxies that could drive inequity.

Transparency & explainability

  • In‑product recommendations include reason codes.
  • We publish model cards detailing data sources, limitations and update cadence.
  • AI‑generated content is clearly labeled throughout training materials.

Human oversight & accountability

  • Human reviewers curate scenario content and feedback rubrics.
  • Escalation paths exist for model errors; responsibilities are defined between client, platform and vendors.
  • All scenario versions and message libraries are versioned for MLR traceability.

Doctor‑patient relationship

  • Training is designed to support, not manipulate, HCP judgement.
  • Off‑label promotion is prohibited and risky language is flagged.

Compliance anchors

We align our program with recognized standards and guidelines: HIPAA Privacy Rule, PhRMA Code, FDA promotional guidance, NIST AI RMF, ISO/IEC 27001, ISO/IEC 42001 and OECD AI Principles. These links open in new tabs and point to authoritative sources on privacy, security and responsible AI.

Frequently Asked Questions

What sets ReflectivAI apart?

ReflectivAI combines AI simulations and analytics with subtle, emotionally intelligent coaching cues — all within strict life-sciences compliance guardrails.

Does it replace human coaching?

No. ReflectivAI scales practice, insights, and certification; managers and trainers remain essential to interpret data and personalize development.

How do you handle PHI?

PHI is off by default. When enabled, we operate under HIPAA controls (minimum-necessary scope, encryption in transit/at rest, audit logging) and a signed BAA[1].

Is it customizable by disease state and region?

Yes. Modules, objections, and labeling align to your therapeutic areas and local regulations.

Can it integrate with LMS/CRM?

Absolutely. Export packs and APIs are available for Salesforce, Veeva, and leading LMS platforms.
