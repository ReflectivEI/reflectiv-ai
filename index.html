Our Mission

Reflectiv exists to empower the next generation with the self-knowledge and skills needed to thrive in an uncertain world. By weaving social and emotional learning into everyday life and leveraging agentic AI, we help youth understand their emotions, build healthy relationships and make purposeful decisions. To support this journey, we offer validated emotional intelligence assessments to give young people a baseline of their strengths and growth areas and tailor support accordingly.

Self-Awareness

Identify, name and understand emotions through guided journaling and reflective prompts. Knowing what you feel is the first step toward emotional intelligence.

Resilience

Build coping strategies and bounce back from challenges with supportive activities, challenges and feedback informed by agentic AI.

Purpose

Connect your strengths and passions to actions that matter. Reflectiv helps youth explore meaningful goals and align daily choices with their values.

The Reflectiv Approach

Our four-step process makes cultivating emotional intelligence intuitive and engaging.

01

Assess & Reflect

Complete a brief assessment to understand your emotional strengths and growth areas. Begin journaling to discover patterns in your mood and thoughts.

02

Personalized Path

Agentic AI curates opportunities and challenges aligned with your goals—from mindfulness exercises to cooperative projects—so you stay motivated.

03

Daily Habits

Practice simple routines each day: mood check-ins, gratitude journaling and empathy games help turn insight into action.

04

Long-Term Routines

Develop enduring habits that reinforce resilience and self-regulation, supported by progress tracking and periodic reflections.

Evidence-Based Design

Reflectiv is built on scientific research demonstrating the power of social and emotional learning and emerging technologies.

  • Social and emotional learning (SEL) teaches emotional management, resilience and relationship skills and is linked to positive outcomes such as improved emotional intelligence, reduced distress and enhanced academic performance [1].
  • During the 2023-24 school year, 63% of U.S. public schools implemented SEL curricula, and 72% of those schools reported moderate to high effectiveness in improving student outcomes [2].
  • Game-based SEL interventions like Brain Agents show that trauma-informed digital experiences can enhance resilience and emotional intelligence among youth exposed to adversity [3].
  • Agentic AI refers to autonomous systems that plan, reason and act with minimal human oversight [4].
  • AI companions are increasingly common; youth report frequent use and even friendships with chatbots [5].
  • AI literacy matters. Experts note AI cannot replace human connection and learners should evaluate AI outputs critically [6].

Where Reflectiv Is Today — and Where We're Headed

Reflectiv was built with a clear mission: to help youth build emotional intelligence and reflective agency when interacting with AI. Our current capabilities emphasise self-awareness, meta-cognition and responsible decision-making. We’re laying the groundwork for future features that will deepen empathy, stress regulation and ethical collaboration with digital agents.
