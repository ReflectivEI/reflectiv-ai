Ethics, Privacy & Governance

Data privacy & security

  • PHI off by default; training uses synthetic or de-identified data.
  • When enabled, HIPAA BAA, encryption, RBAC, purge on expiration.
  • Vendor risk management with annual reviews and right-to-audit.

Algorithmic fairness

  • Pre-deployment bias tests and periodic re-tests.
  • Monitor disparate performance; remediate and publish changes.
  • Guardrails block non-clinical proxies for targeting.

Transparency

  • Recommendations show reason codes.
  • Model cards document data sources, limits, cadence.
  • AI-generated content labeled in product.

Scoring rubric

How scoring works

  • Accuracy (0–5): factual precision; label-aligned.
  • Compliance (0–5): avoids off-label; fair balance.
  • Discovery (0–5): clear questions driving next step.
  • Objection handling (0–5): addresses barriers safely.
  • Clarity (0–5): concise, one idea per sentence.
  • Empathy (0–5): validates HCP context.

Overall score is a weighted blend of the above with bonuses for ideal length and a closing question.
