Our Mission

Reflectiv exists to empower the next generation with the self‑knowledge and skills needed to thrive in an uncertain world. By weaving social and emotional learning into everyday life and leveraging agentic AI, we help youth understand their emotions, build healthy relationships and make purposeful decisions. To support this journey, we offer validated emotional intelligence assessments to give young people a baseline of their strengths and growth areas and tailor support accordingly.

Self‑Awareness

Identify, name and understand emotions through guided journaling and reflective prompts. Knowing what you feel is the first step toward emotional intelligence.

Resilience

Build coping strategies and bounce back from challenges with supportive activities, challenges and feedback informed by agentic AI.

Purpose

Connect your strengths and passions to actions that matter. Reflectiv helps youth explore meaningful goals and align daily choices with their values.

Evidence‑Based Design

Reflectiv is built on scientific research demonstrating the power of social and emotional learning and emerging technologies.

  • Social and emotional learning (SEL) teaches emotional management, resilience and relationship skills and is linked to positive outcomes such as improved emotional intelligence, reduced distress and enhanced academic performance [1].
  • During the 2023‑24 school year, 63% of U.S. public schools implemented SEL curricula, and 72% of those schools reported moderate to high effectiveness in improving student outcomes [2].
  • Game‑based SEL interventions like Brain Agents demonstrate that trauma‑informed digital experiences can enhance resilience and emotional intelligence among youth exposed to adversity. Video games integrated with SEL content help students regulate emotions, develop empathy and improve mental health [3].
  • Agentic AI refers to autonomous systems that plan, reason and act with minimal human oversight [4]. Integrating agentic AI into Reflectiv means your personalized journey evolves intelligently with your progress and context.
  • AI companions are increasingly common: a 2025 report found that 72% of teens have used AI chatbots for companionship and 33% describe having friendships with them [5]. Reflectiv teaches self‑awareness and boundaries in digital relationships.
  • AI literacy matters: experts note that while AI agents can support learning, they cannot fully replicate human connection. Children should be taught to understand the limitations of AI and to critically evaluate AI‑generated content [6]. Reflectiv integrates AI literacy tools so youth can engage safely and ethically with agentic AI.
